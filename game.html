CDL index player info What roles do and Rules

Roles

The main ar role is to be the back anchor of the team and make sure nothing is out of place by the team. The main ar is usually the igl(in game leader) The flex role is a hybrid of ar and sub, a player that can run both, and be the deciding factor of when the player wants to use either one. The flex is the second ar, but much faster than the main ar. The flex player will usually be the main vocal point of the team when trying to make play calls. The sub role is similar to the flex, but will use a smg more often, but can occassionally run the ar depending on the map. The main sub player is the most aggressive player on the team, and will always have the most engagments on the team. The sub player has alot of roles to do depending what the team needs from him. Overall, the perfect team will do whatever they need to do to win. Sometimes the main ar will do what a sub player does, but will go back to the main main ar objective.

Game Modes

Hardpoint: Hardpoint is a gamemode of 5 hills on the map, and each hill be active for 60 seconds. The team to 250 first, or if neither team hits 250, then the team with the most points will win.

SnD: search and destroy is attackers vs defenders. The attackers can win the round by either killing the other team fully, or planting the bomb and having the bomb reach zero before it can be defused. The defenders can win a round by killing the other team, or defusing the bomb in 7.5 secs before the timer hits 0. The teams will flip sides until one team hits 6 round wins.

control: control is a gamemode where both teams have 30 lives, and it is split between defenders and attackers. The defenders win by having the time run out, or eliminating the other team 30 lives. The attackers can win by capturing points A and B. They can also win by eliminating the other teams 30 lives.
